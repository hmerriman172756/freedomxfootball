Payouts

Top 25% of players in each division split the total pot, with 1st place winners earning larger shares. In case of a points tie at the end of the 18-week season, the tiebreaker is the player who was in the lead for the most weeks during the season.

🏈Fan Division

100 players × $250 = $25,000 pot. Approx: 1st: $3,750, 2nd: $2,625, 3rd: $1,875, 4th-10th: $625 each, 11th-25th: $250 each.

🏈Semi-Pro Division

100 players × $500 = $50,000 pot. Approx: 1st: $7,500, 2nd: $5,250, 3rd: $3,750, 4th-10th: $1,250 each, 11th-25th: $500 each.

🏈Pro Player Division
