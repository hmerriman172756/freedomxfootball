Scoring Rules

  • 11 Point – Straight-Up Win
    Pick a team to win, no spread. Popular but low reward.
    Win: +1 point
  • 22 Points – Win With the Spread
    Pick a team to win or cover the spread.
    Example: Underdog +7 loses by <7 or wins: +2 points
  • 33 Points – Underdog Wins Straight-Up
    Pick an underdog to win outright, no spread. High risk, high reward.
    Win: +3 points
