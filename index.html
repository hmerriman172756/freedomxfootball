Scoring Rules

🚨 No points are awarded for ties in any scoring category! 🚨

  • 1 1 Point – Straight-Up Win
    Pick a team to win, no spread. Popular but low reward.
    Tie: 0 points
    Win: +1 point
  • 2 2 Points – Win With the Spread
    Pick a team to win or cover the spread.
    Example: Underdog +7 loses by 3, you win as they "covered" the +7 spread.
    Tie: 0 points
    Win: +2 points
  • 3 3 Points – Underdog Wins Straight-Up
    Pick an underdog to win outright, no spread. High risk, high reward.
    Tie: 0 points
    Win: +3 points

Payouts

In case of a points tie at the end of the 18-week season, the tiebreaker is the player who was in the lead for the most weeks during the season.

  • 1st Place: Receives 25% of the total pot.
  • 2nd Place: Receives a smaller share of the pot.
  • 3rd Place: Receives a smaller share of the pot.
  • 4th-10th Place: Receives a smaller share of the pot.
  • 11th-25th Place: Receives a smaller share of the pot.

*Payouts are distributed via Venmo after each season, based on the exact distribution formula.
