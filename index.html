Payouts

Top 50% of players in each division split 75% of the total pot, with higher scorers earning larger shares. In case of a points tie, the player with the most wins is the tiebreaker. If wins are equal, the player who was in the lead more during the season prevails.

Example Payouts (100 Players per Division)

  • Fan Division: 100 players × $250 = $25,000 pot. Top 50 split 75% ($18,750). Approx: 1st: $938, 2nd: $900, ..., 50th: $94.
  • Semi-Pro Division: 100 players × $500 = $50,000 pot. Top 50 split 75% ($37,500). Approx: 1st: $1,875, 2nd: $1,800, ..., 50th: $188.
  • Pro Player Division: 100 players × $1,000 = $100,000 pot. Top 50 split 75% ($75,000). Approx: 1st: $3,750, 2nd: $3,600, ..., 50th: $375.

*Example assumes top 50 players per division with a tiered distribution (highest scorer gets largest share, decreasing to 50th). Actual payouts depend on the exact distribution formula.

Scoring Rules

  • 1 Point – Straight-Up Win
    Pick a team to win, no spread. Popular but low reward.
    Win: +1 point
  • 2 Points – Win With the Spread
    Pick a team to win or cover the spread.
    Example: Underdog +7 loses by <7 or wins; no points for a tie
  • 3 Points – Underdog Wins Straight-Up
    Pick an underdog to win outright, no spread. High risk, high reward.
    Win: +3 points
